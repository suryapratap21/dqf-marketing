A Driver Qualification File is a federally mandated record that every commercial motor carrier must maintain for each CDL driver under 49 CFR Parts 382 and 391. It includes the driver's application, motor vehicle record, road test certificate, medical certificate, prior employment history, and annual reviews. Prime DQ Files digitizes and automates the assembly of all five compliance sections.

We run MVR (Motor Vehicle Records), PSP (Pre-Employment Screening Program), CDLIS (Commercial Driver's License Information System), and Federal Clearinghouse queries in parallel. Running these simultaneously cuts typical screening time from several days to a matter of hours.

Every driver receives a score from 0–100 that maps to one of four risk tiers: A (low risk, fast-track hire), B (acceptable), C (review required), or D (disqualified). The score is calculated from driving history, violations, background check results, and years of experience. The model is fully transparent and defensible in any DOT audit or litigation.
