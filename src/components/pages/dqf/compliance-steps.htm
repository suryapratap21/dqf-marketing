Requirements

The 5 compliance areas Prime DQ Files covers — automatically.

A complete DQF requires documentation across five federal categories. We automate the collection, verification, and archiving of all of them.

49 CFR §391.21

Driver Application

The signed driver application is the foundation of every DQF. Drivers complete and e-sign it digitally during onboarding.

  • 10-year employment history
  • CDL and license information
  • Accident & violation history

49 CFR §391.23

Background Checks

Federal law requires MVR, PSP, CDLIS, and Federal Clearinghouse queries. We order all four in parallel and import results automatically.

  • Motor Vehicle Record (MVR)
  • PSP — Pre-Employment Screening
  • CDLIS & Clearinghouse

49 CFR §391.31

Road Test & Certificate

Road test certificates must be retained for each driver or a valid CDL may be substituted. We capture and store both.

  • Digital road test certificate upload
  • CDL substitution validation
  • Examiner signature capture

49 CFR §391.41–45

Medical Certification

Drivers must hold a valid DOT medical certificate from a certified examiner. We track expiration dates and alert you before they lapse.

  • Medical certificate upload & storage
  • Expiration date tracking
  • 30/60/90-day renewal alerts

49 CFR §391.25

Annual Review

Carriers must review each driver's driving record annually. We automate the review trigger, document the review, and keep a timestamped record.

  • Automated annual review reminder
  • MVR pull & comparison
  • Supervisor sign-off capture

Drug & Alcohol · 49 CFR Part 382

Federal Clearinghouse — handled.

The FMCSA Drug & Alcohol Clearinghouse requires a query for every new hire and an annual query for all active drivers. We automate both — including consent collection from drivers.
