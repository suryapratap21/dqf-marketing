Our Mission

To make DOT compliance effortless for every carrier.

Compliance failures aren't a carrier problem — they're a paperwork problem. Most carriers are doing everything right but lose audits because documents are missing, expired, or disorganized.

We built Prime DQ Files to eliminate that gap. Our platform automates every step of the Driver Qualification File process — from driver onboarding to audit-ready export.

  • Compliance-first architecture — built around 49 CFR Part 391
  • Automated background checks — no manual ordering or follow-up
  • Audit-ready PDF export in seconds, not hours
  • SOC 2 Type II certified, FMCSA-aligned data practices
