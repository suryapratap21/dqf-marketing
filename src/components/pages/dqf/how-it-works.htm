How It Works

From invite to audit-ready in days.

Three steps. No paperwork. No follow-up emails. A complete, DOT-compliant Driver Qualification File at the end.

Step 01

Invite the driver.

Send a secure onboarding link. The driver completes their application, e-signs required forms, and uploads documents — all from their phone.

Step 02

Checks run in parallel.

MVR, PSP, CDLIS, and Federal Clearinghouse queries fire automatically and simultaneously — no manual follow-up or waiting on each one separately.

Step 03

Close the DQF.

Review the risk score, approve the hire, and export a complete, timestamped, audit-ready PDF — ready for any DOT inspection in seconds.
